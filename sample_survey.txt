# Format instructions:
#   SECTION:      Section title in English
#   SECTION_AR:   Section title in Arabic
#   QUESTION:     Question text in English
#   QUESTION_AR:  Question text in Arabic
#   TYPE:         single_choice | multiple_choice | rating | text
#   OPTIONS:      Pipe-separated options in English  (for choice types)
#   OPTIONS_AR:   Pipe-separated options in Arabic   (for choice types)
#   REQUIRED:     yes (default) | no
# ============================================================

SECTION: Work Environment
SECTION_AR: بيئة العمل

QUESTION: How do you rate the overall work environment in your department?
QUESTION_AR: كيف تقيم بيئة العمل العامة في قسمك؟
TYPE: rating
REQUIRED: yes

QUESTION: How do you rate communication and collaboration between departments?
QUESTION_AR: كيف تقيم مستوى التواصل والتعاون بين الأقسام؟
TYPE: single_choice
OPTIONS: Excellent|Very Good|Good|Needs Improvement|Poor
OPTIONS_AR: ممتاز|جيد جداً|جيد|يحتاج تحسين|ضعيف
REQUIRED: yes

QUESTION: What are the main challenges you face in the work environment?
QUESTION_AR: ما هي أبرز التحديات التي تواجهها في بيئة العمل؟
TYPE: multiple_choice
OPTIONS: Work Pressure|Lack of Resources|Poor Communication|Technical Challenges|Management Relations|Other
OPTIONS_AR: ضغط العمل|قلة الموارد|ضعف التواصل|التحديات التقنية|العلاقة مع الإدارة|أخرى
REQUIRED: no
